Pub/sub demo — 1 publisher, 4 subscribers

One component publishes message.posted when you click "Send". Four subscribers react independently: a feed lists every message, a counter aggregates statistics, a card shows the latest, a histogram tracks activity per second. Zero network. Zero mocks. Just GlueZero coordinating components inside the same page.

Library: @gluezero/gluezero@1.0.1 Loaded from: esm.sh Topic used: message.posted Backend: none (pure pub/sub)
